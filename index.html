Privacy Policy

HVAC Piping & Refrigerant Calculator · Effective July 19, 2026

This app is a field-service calculator for HVAC technicians. It is built to work fully offline, and it is designed so that your data stays on your device.

Data we collect

None. The app has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking. The developer does not receive, collect, store, or sell any personal information or usage data.

Data stored on your device

Everything you create in the app — saved calculation jobs, inspection records, exported programming sheet history, entered access codes, in-progress drafts, and settings — is stored locally on your device only. It is never transmitted to the developer or to any third party. Deleting the app deletes this data.

Camera

The optional dataplate scanner uses your camera to photograph an equipment dataplate. Text recognition runs entirely on your device. Photos are processed locally, are not uploaded anywhere, and are not saved to your photo library by the app. The camera is used only when you tap a Scan button, and the app works fully without granting camera permission.

Sharing and PDFs

PDF reports and programming sheets are generated on your device. They leave your device only when you choose to share them through your phone's share sheet (email, AirDrop, messaging, etc.). Where those PDFs go is entirely under your control.

Network use

All calculations, reference tables, and lookups are bundled with the app and work with no internet connection. The app may download app updates through the standard app store / update mechanisms; no personal data is sent as part of this.

Children

This is a professional trade tool and is not directed at children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If the app's data practices ever change, this page will be updated and the effective date above revised.

Contact

Questions or feedback: email hvacpipingcalc@gmail.com or use the Contact Support button on the app's Settings page.
